New project of ReactOS Foundation

Our russian department launched a new service for opensource projects hosting: FireForge.net. This site provides file releases, SVN/CVS, web, ftp hosting, bug/feature trackers, mailing lists and everything else needed for building a successive opensource community. A difference from similar known sites like sf.net or code.google.com is that FireForge.net provides hosting services for projects interested for russian audience, and it's not just about providing services, but it's about providing a way for new projects to become known quicker, attract interested users and developers.

The website is also available in english and some other languages.
