"Soft of the Year 2008" Award

"Soft of the Year 2008" is a national Russian software award given annually to the best computer software. Winners in seven different categories are selected by results of people's voting (carried out by Mail.Ru).

ReactOS won second place in the "Technologies" category. Thanks to everyone who supported us!
