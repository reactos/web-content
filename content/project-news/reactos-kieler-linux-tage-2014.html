--- title: "ReactOS at Kieler Linux Tage 2014" author: "Z98" date: 2014-08-11 aliases: [ "node/867" ] ---

The ReactOS Project will be at the Kieler Linux Tage convention, running September 19th to the 20th in Kiel. Several of the developers will be present and Colin Finck will be presenting the project at a talk on the 20th at 11AM local time. So stop by if you are in the area and say hi to the developers.
