--- title: "PeaZIP Joins Open Source Support Program" author: "Z98" date: 2014-05-28 aliases: [ "/node/835" ] ---

The ReactOS Project is pleased another participant in the Open Source Support Program, PeaZIP.

Many community members have been asking that some sort of zip utility be bundled with ReactOS for convenience and while participation in the program does not mean PeaZIP will be present by default, it will make it considerably easier for users to pre-stage PeaZIP so that it will be available to use once the installation of ReactOS is complete.

PeaZIP supports a wide range of archive formats such as 7Z, RAR, TAR, and of course ZIP, and many more. With that support, users that pre-stage it in ReactOS will likely be able to open basically any archive they ever have a need to.
